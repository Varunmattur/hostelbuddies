Hostel Buddy

About Us

Hostel Buddy is a platform created for students living in hostels to buy and sell items that are no longer in use. Our mission is to help students save money and make hostel life more comfortable by connecting them with others who need what they no longer use.

Whether you need a used study table or want to sell your old fridge, Hostel Buddy is the ideal platform for you. We aim to provide a safe and easy-to-use environment where students can exchange items they no longer need.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a community where students can help each other by exchanging resources that make their hostel lives more convenient and affordable. Hostel Buddy aims to be the go-to platform for hostel-based transactions.
